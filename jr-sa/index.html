Junior Solutions Architect Program Overview

The Junior Solutions Architect (JR SA) Program provides an opportunity for recent college graduates to learn the fundamentals of how to be technical leaders in Red Hat technologies. JR SAs will receive training on Red Hat’s core platforms and ever growing technology portfolio, and they will have the opportunity to complete Red Hat certifications, including Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA) and Red Hat Certified Engineer (RHCE).

Upon successful completion of the 18-24 month program, JR SAs may be considered for an Associate Solutions Architect role and continue their careers supporting the greater sales organization and/or other technical teams at Red Hat.

Selected candidates will begin this program in July 2021.

What is a Solutions Architect?

An SA is a technical, customer-facing individual with deep knowledge of Red Hat products and technology who is responsible for architecting solutions and representing Red Hat’s business portfolio in support of the Red Hat Sales Team as necessary for customers and partners in a broad range of engagements.

For more information, send your contact information and/or resume to jr-sa@redhat.com
